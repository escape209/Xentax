Updated here

Code: Select all

    FC5.Unpacker <m_FATFile> <m_Directory>

    m_FATFile - Source of FAT file
    m_Directory - Destination directory

[Examples]
    FC5.Unpacker D:\FC5\data_final\pc\common.fat D:\Unpacked\common
