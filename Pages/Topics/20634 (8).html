Post by Sharppy » Sat May 22, 2021 12:15 pm

The list you have to modify

I put it as :

spiderman.exe [hash]

I dont have the list anymore since its easy to make. Use googledoc sheets to add the column for "spiderman.exe" just download as comma separated .csv
Then for the 1st comma CTRL+R for replace the "," with "spiderman.exe" with a space so "spiderman.exe ".
Then you can just run the tool from CMD do CTRL+A and CTRL+C all the info in the new list. and CTRL+V into the CMD and process all the models at once. That's the best way to use daemons tools.
