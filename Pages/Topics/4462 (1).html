I don't know if people still care about this, but I literally created an account to post.

Years ago, I came across this version of FFmpeg that could decode .xmv so they could be played and converted to other formats. It said it worked with this specific video converter called Avanti, in its GUI variant. Don't ask me where I found them, as I have no memory of this.

Long story short, this program, when used with the right version of FFmpeg, is able to convert .xmv files into other more user-friendly formats like .avi and .mp4.

http://www.mediafire.com/file/olyd9plmg ... UI-075.zip

It's ready to be used. I tried it with .xmv videos from the Dance Dance Revolution Ultramix series and it worked like a charm.
