Well, there is one way. And even then it's a pain in the butt -- in 3DS Max, for example, there's a way to export the "envelopes" for a model segment and re-import it, re-assigning the original envelopes for different bones. Only problem with that, though, is that separate elements may need to be manually fixed even then (Rin's sleeve computer, for instance).
