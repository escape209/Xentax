Post by sf234 » Sun Apr 05, 2015 8:59 am

DLC Tool adapted to PC Format. Apart from previous requirements (listed here: viewtopic.php?p=101431#p101431):
[*]DLC name must be only numerical.
[*]Corresponding entry must be added to steam_api.ini (e.g. for DLC 112233 it would be DLC00*=112233 (* = any free number)).
viewtopic.php?p=104965#p104965
Rosalin wrote:sf234it works fine, but it seems that you didn't reversed key values in blp and lnk.
I only changed keys search algorithm without looking to other parts of code (handling of endianness was added by mikulover39). Maybe later.
MisterNatal wrote:Kind of stupid to ask, but what do I do with the information that i've just "copied to clipboard" insert it into the LR archivarius?
Just open doa5LRarchivarius.dat with any text editor and paste it.
