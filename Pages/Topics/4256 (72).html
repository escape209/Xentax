AirOne wrote:Please find attached FM3+4 car list with tires specs and rims coordinates (646 cars without duplicates).
AirOne, I take a look to your file, and I can’t identify every fields.
I guess that 0xyz, 1xyz, 2xyz, 3xyz are the rim’s coordinates, 4xyz maybe a scale factor, but I don’t know what the other values are.
Could you please tell me what is the meaning of each field (0x,0y,0z,1x, ….., 9z)?

edit :
I tested on FM3 R8v10 2010 and i found this :
5xyz : front left wheel's coordinates
6xyz : front right wheel's coordinates
7xyz : rear right wheel's coordinates
8xyz : rear left wheel's coordinates
(invert y and z coordinates into 3ds max)
theses coordinates also work for calipers and rotors
