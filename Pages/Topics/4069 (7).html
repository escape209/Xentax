Yeah, the green maps are masking textures. What I did was to open that texture in photoshop, look in the RGB channels and looked for a blackn' white texture, then copy it and paste it in a new channel called alpha in the hair texture.

Here is some tip to make an alpha map.

1.Step
Open image with photoshop. and turn it Grayscale.




2.Step
Use Brigtness/contrast on adjustments tap





3.Step
Apply Filter - Blur - Gaussian Blur (It seems like fake Anti-Aliasing)





4.Step
Use in 3ds Max - Material - Opercity
