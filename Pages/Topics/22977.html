Hex2obj will give you a point cloud only. Getting the face indices requires additional coding which I did "on the fly":

Code: Select all

# 
f 1 5 4 
f 2 3 6 
f 2 6 5 
f 3 2 1 
f 6 4 5 
f 7 8 12 
f 12 11 7 
f 8 10 14 
f 14 12 8 
f 10 9 13 
f 13 14 10 
f 9 7 11 
f 11 13 9 
f 15 17 18 
f 18 16 15 
f 19 20 22 
f 22 21 19 
f 15 16 20 
f 20 19 15 
f 16 18 22 
f 22 20 16 
f 18 17 21 
f 21 22 18 
f 17 15 19 
f 19 21 17 
f 23 24 28 
f 28 27 23 
#f 24 26 30 
#f 30 28 24 
#f 26 25 29 
#f 29 30 26 
f 25 23 27 
#f 27 29 25
