As for the projection, i came up with an idea.
Suppose a vector (x0,y0,z0) in the origin scene turned out to be (x,y,z) in the ripped scene.
taking it for an example, the COP(centre of projection) is at (x',y',z'),
then:
x0 = a*(x - x')
y0 = b*(y - y')/(x - x')
z0 = c*(z - z')/(x - x')
It's simple.
