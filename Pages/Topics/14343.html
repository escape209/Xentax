Post by Researchman » Thu May 19, 2016 2:33 am

.BNK archives contains ADPCM files.

To decode them, need to use latest ima_rejigger
For mass convertion:

Code: Select all

for /r %%i In (*.wem) do "%CD%/ima_rejigger5.exe" %%i %%i.wav
