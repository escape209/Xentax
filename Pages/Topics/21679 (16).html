Post by Yretenai » Wed Jan 27, 2021 2:15 am

Build 82 fixes support for Ryza 2 PAK archives.
To extract you MUST provide --reisalin-keyfix as an argument.
i.e.

Code: Select all

Cethleann.DataExporter.exe --reisalin D:\Ryza2Data "D:\Steam\steamapps\common\Atelier Ryza 2\Data" --reisalin-keyfix
