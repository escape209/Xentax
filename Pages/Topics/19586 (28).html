First import the animation into Blender Before confirming, check this box

Make a new frame

Import the animation you will have the skeleton Then import your model Select the rotation of the skeleton Rotation X 90 For it to be positioned on the model press A to select everything Once everything is selected, do a CTRL P and choose Armature Deform Now your model and well animated I hope to have helped you

Hair animation with Wiggle Bone

To animate the hair select the smd points of the hair Check the box Wiggle Bone

Play the animation with animated hair

I did it on my Blender 2.83.1 and it works perfectly
