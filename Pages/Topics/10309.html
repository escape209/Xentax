Post by DCxDemo » Sat Apr 06, 2013 5:09 pm

Pitbull DAT Splitter is a simple extractor for DAT pseudo-archives that can be found in several games, build on PS1 engine by Pitbull Syndicate.

This includes:
Test Drive 4
Test Drive 5
Test Drive 6
Demolition Racer
Big Air
possibly the Test Drive Off-Road series

Download tool: http://www.mediafire.com/download.php?qg1scdd1ruicwfu

Please share if you'll find that this tool works for some other games.
