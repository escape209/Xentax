Post by Mr.Mouse » Wed May 23, 2012 10:45 pm

Interesting discussion. It seems the inconvenience for you is more important than abiding to a few laws. My estimate is 80+ games are released on various platforms each month, with many of them having downloadable demos for anyone to download legally. I understand your troubles, but you do not see ours. Honestly, if you feel so bad about this all, and do not wish to come to a workable situation because it causes so much problems for you, then you must make your own decision.

Aluigi has earned a lot of respect, and he is a great guy. The early history of the board lies in 2003 however, when I set this up, with Craptain, and Watto. Where Watto and I would do all the analysing, posting scripts, program updates and such. That was the start and was the situation for some years. Many others came, including aluigi, with his great quickbms fork of mexscript, and his relentless talent. There is also no doubt about Alpha.

And then we have many more talented people here and each contributes to a fine community where no file needs to go unravelled. But we have to find the workable balance between what is in the grey area, and what is downright illegal. Again, I'm sorry aluigi drops the bomb on us. But what we will not be is a warez site. And what we will not pursue is pre-release file analysis. And we will stop a type of conduct that we once thought was possible or cool, but now seems to be causing more problems than it solves.

Like MrAdults said: we can do a lot more, than just prompting scripts for each sample uploaded. If that is not your game, and you cannot or simply will not understand where we are coming from, then that is a disappointment.
