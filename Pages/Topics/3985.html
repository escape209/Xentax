Post by Evin » Mon Dec 28, 2009 9:05 am

I made translation to PoP: Warrior Within, where is almost everything subtitled. In SOT and T2T, just the tutorial and the menu.
In popww (the modified JADE engine almost the same in the first 2 games), the fonts are in menu....bin files, the texts are in fd...bin files. The bin files are compressed, and no repacker. So you need to set the new filesize etc. And if you make 1! mistake, the game wont start. This is good and bad thing.
(I had 2 weeks to fix every problem, the translation was 2days with checking)
