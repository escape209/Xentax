Thanks Mr. Mouse and Craptain for doing what you can to help keep this thread on topic, it is much appreciated.

The internet is not some virtual world where you can do and say whatever the hell you want without any regard for others. This topic has always been about the research, and I would like to continue doing so in this thread, provided that people maintain proper levels of respect for others in this community. Please remember that those who contribute to this community do not owe anybody anything. They do it in their own free time because they simly love doing so. Do not ruin it for us.

That being said, I've made a little bit more progress last night and this morning mapping out track objects. It is currently only rendering the first piece in each object, so some may still look a bit incomplete, and I haven't cared to pinpoint normals for each of the different vertex formats that they use yet, so some of the lighting may seem pretty weird as well.

The ultimate goal here is to get the track mesh and objects extracted to have a nice scene to render the cars on. I currently have no plans on extracting much of anything else and definitely do not plan on re-importing them into Forza or any other game so please do not bother me with such requests. So far I've mapped out past the filetable on the main track files (.stx.bin) with no real sight of the mesh data yet. I will be sure to post my updated project's source once I've figured a little more about it all.
