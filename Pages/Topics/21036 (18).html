OK SO I ACTUALLY FOUND A WAY TO REPLACE THE NEWER HORSE TEXTURES!! It's not even that hard!

What You'll Need: Star Stable Online - Pimp My Horse - SSO Extract - Photo Editing Software That Can Open .DDS Format


Step 1: Extract everything converted using SSO Extract (It can take a while)

Step 2: Search GH

Step 3: Find the texture of the horse you wanna change

Step 4: Open the .DDS in Photo Editing Software

Step 5: Edit it how you want

Step 6: Size it a little (I usually size it to 300 by 300 but I think you can go higher than that for more detail)

Step 8: Save it somewhere on your Desktop

Step 9: Open Pimp My Horse

Step 10: Click "Choose Horse From List"

Step 11: Find your Starter Horse texture

Step 12: Click "Confirm"

Step 13: Find the PackFiles folder (It should be C:\Program Files (x86)\Star Stable Online\PackFiles )

Step 14: Paste it in where it says "PackFiles Folder:"

Steo 15: Click "Choose Your DDS File"

Step 16: Click the texture you saved somewhere on your Desktop

Step 17: Click "Change Body in SSO"

Step 18: Log on to Star Stable

Step 19: Ride your Starter Horse

Step 20: Go to the Jarlaheim Bus Stop

Step 21: Sit on the bench closest to the Bus Stop

Step 22: Go to the Mall

Step 23: Leave the Mall

Step 24: Go to a shop

Step 25: Stand up

Step 26: Go to your Home Stable

Step 27: Sit down in the chair at your Home Stable

Step 28: Close the shop

Step 29: Ride your Starter Horse

Step 30: Change Starter Horse Appearance

Step 31: Stand up

Step 32: Ride any horse out of the Home Stable

Step 33: Go back in the Home Stable

Step 34: Ride the horse you want to change the texture of

Step 35: Click Reset

Then it should have the custom texture!!! I hope this helped :3
