DisFonts.upk — contains Chalet Comprime and Emerge fonts as ttf imported to GFX lib. You can modify libs with FFDEC.
UI_Loading_SF_LOC_INT.upk — contains Chalet Comprime and Emerge fonts as image + binary map.
