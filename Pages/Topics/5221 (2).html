Tosyk wrote:It don't work, why?

Well, Falo's dumper has become outdated. Yaz0r and kkdf2's dumpers work much better for KH2FM now, and Yaz0r's nametable is full of most of the files, except a few anbs in the eh and eh_l folders like Roxas's keys and Ansem's Guardian, and a few other files that are hard to snuff out. Use those from now on; yaz0r's initially to grab most of the files, and kkdf2's expack1e to grab any specific files.
