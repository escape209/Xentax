Id suggest using "custom texture tool" from deadray website https://deadray.com/detroit/files/Detro ... v0.1.1.zip (link is dead sometimes but try refreshing)
Just use search box to find whatever you need.
ARC_ ids are locations, ACC_ - props, CHA_ - characters etc etc
Once you know the ID (on my screenshot its 1B E5 (4 symbols after 00 00 00 1D 00 00 00 01 00 00) you can use tool from first post of this thread to get models and textures.
