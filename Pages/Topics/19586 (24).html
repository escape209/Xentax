dixen18 wrote: ↑Tue Jun 23, 2020 11:03 am Somebody know how delete NON-russian and NON-english voice *.wem inside .d## files?
What do you mean with .d## files? From what I know you can paste this into a batch file and run it in the folder with the sound files:
for %%f in (*fre.wem, *ger.wem, *ita.wem, *mex.wem, *pol.wem, *por.wem, *spa.wem, *ara.wem, *bra.wem, *jpn.wem) do del %%f
I'm sure there's a way do delete everything that doesn't include *rus.wem and *eng.wem. But I did it that way.
