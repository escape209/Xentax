Post by Mr.Mouse » Fri Jul 08, 2005 3:13 pm

Okay, the sound.dat that doesn't work has a different format. There's the same type of table at the back showing filenames and offsets and sizes, but the sizes and offsets do not match the actual files in there. Looks like the sounds have been enhanced (they're bigger in general that what they should be). The titles of the sounds do fit them.

For now, you can open them using for instance the No One Lives Forever .REZ format. Just type *.* after you selected those and you can point to the .DAT file. They have no names, but you can extract them anyway.
