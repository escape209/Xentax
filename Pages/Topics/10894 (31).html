Post by shakotay2 » Tue Feb 14, 2023 5:35 am

Use File/SaveAs mesh to get the combined model (vertices and uvs) as a wavefront.obj file.

H2O file, which you may have created already:

0x1462 1106
Vb1
24 20
0x22 216
020100
0x0 255
