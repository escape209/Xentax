Basically what I suggested above - a Frontpage Item Discussion forum where each item has its own discussion thread, with the item being repeated in the first post, and the thread being linked from the actual frontpage item. After that, disable comments on the front page to keep all discussion on-wiki. Guests should be able to post to the actual forum, IMHO; spam can be very effectively controlled via the use of reCAPTCHA.
