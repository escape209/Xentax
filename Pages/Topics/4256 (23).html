Post by Simon » Thu Apr 29, 2010 7:06 pm

Okay but you can't do much without this tool...

And sorry that I don't post a step by step Tutorial...

Howto:

Open one Texture you like with a Hex Editor.

Insert 60 Bytes at the beginning and Drop the Attached Header there.

Go to Offset 0x8 and Change the 6 Bytes to the Filesize WITH the 60 Extra Bytes. (It's in Big Endian.) This header has 16 00 70 = 1.441.904 Bytes. Important if you don't do that it will Crash.

Then go to Offset 0x20 and change the Texture Name to your liking.

Then UnBundler.exe texturename

It outputs all Mipmaps of the Texture.
