Ninjaripper will help you get rip model quick and easy. I recommend you learn hex2obj, or Modelresearcher. Hex2obj took me a few days to wrap my head around, and that's coming from a noob. Of course there are tools like Quickbms, Max, Blender, and Noesis with plenty of premade scripts. If you want to have even more control, then you need to use a bit of programming, good luck
