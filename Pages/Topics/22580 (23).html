Post by s373ns » Sun Jul 02, 2023 1:59 pm

I've come across these pretty frequently. One thing I've discovered is that the real texture exists in another pak. For example, the tarp on the logs outside Ellie's garage has the 'missing material' texture on it in most of the paks, but I imported the 'epilogue' pak of the same garage, and the tarp had the correct texture. I believe if there's a related pak to the one you're using, you should be able to find the same mesh with the correct material.
