Post by AirOne » Tue Oct 11, 2011 1:18 pm

FM4AutoUnpacker

http://www.deviantart.com/download/2628 ... 4chzx1.zip

Extract "FM4AutoUnpacker" on "C:\".
Copy "quickbms.exe", "zip.bms" and "FM4cars.bat" into FM4 cars directory (\Media\cars).
Launch "FM4cars.bat".
All cars will be extracted into "C:\FM4AutoUnpacker\FM4cars" directory.
Copy "quickbms.exe", "zip.bms" and "FM4wheels.bat" into FM4 cars directory (\Media\wheels).
Launch "FM4wheels.bat".
All wheels will be extracted into "C:\FM4AutoUnpacker\FM4wheels" directory.

Enjoy!
