You have it here first folks
https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/131 ... %201.0.rar
The archives themselves can be opened with BloodRayne tools, since the engine is the same, but after that you are left with unknown files which cannot be opened with those
Cities and skinned characters are stored in different format, and I got bored at that point, but all cars, textures and interactieve objects in .TXD and .SMB format are mostly parsable, have fun, anyone, besides me I guess, any roadkill design enjoyers?
Attachments
RoadkillParser 1.0.rar
only converts textures and models, you'll need other tools to extract them, full sources included
