uv.Y = v = 1.0f - v;

Some vertex declarations used have multiple uv coordinates, as well as extra position values for the damage morphs. i was going to attempt playing around with them hen i got the chance, so i added the extra variables. Hadn't gotten to cleaning things up yet.
