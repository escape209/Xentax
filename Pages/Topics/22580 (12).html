Post by kotn3l » Wed Apr 21, 2021 5:36 pm

Jan666 wrote: ↑Sun Apr 18, 2021 11:32 am i think your tool is working, but how i connect the animtion to the mesh/ sekl in the correct way
All the helper bones needs to be a child of the corresponding bone. For example: R_thigh_helper should be a child of R_thigh etc. By launching the tool from a command line, you must give it the /r switch, so it will automatically reparent the helper bones when extracting. Or you can do it manually....thats what I did before.
