looks like you'll need a reverse tristrips algorithm.
vertices:
A
B
C
D
E
F

triangles:
(1)ABC
(2)..BCD
(3)....CDE
(4)......DEF

So you start backwards from (4) F,E,D
take C from (3), B from (2) and A from (1)

That is reverse A,B,C,D,E,F

HTH
