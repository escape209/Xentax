I've been seeing a lot of questions recently regarding the functionality of these new builds so I figure I'll pop in real quick and clear up some of the confusion.

In case I didn't make myself clear earlier, all recent "releases" of Forza Studio (newer than 7.9.10) are unofficial builds I've literally hacked together in order to give you something to play with until I have the time to work out a proper version. I figured this was preferred to no release at all, but I'm beginning to regret this decision.

As for what they can do...I've completely stripped Forza 3 support from them so they should only be able to open Forza 4 LOD0 carbin files (interior/exterior/autovista) at the moment, and I cannot guarantee any functionality, as I've only examined a few carbin files during my development and there are likely special cases which need to be handled accordingly.

I'm unsure as to when I'll get the time to fix UVs or add support for other things, and if there are any other questions, feel free to ask...
