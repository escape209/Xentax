AlexMH3 wrote:How do I download Unpakke? The link in the 1st post doesn't work for me.

Also, I'm trying to use it to extract the RS2.$hd$.bik.BF file from Red Steel 2 for the Wii.
Online research showed that Unpakke could extract .BF files.

The URL should work.

About the .BF file, keep in mind that the extension doesn't mean anything. Unpakke can unpack with a 100% success only the package files from games listed on the Unpakke's homepage.

.BIK should stand for Bink Video video file from RAD game tools, unless it's something else, sharing the same extension.

Good luck!
