Okay, so I did some more digging around and I found that the sounds are 22050 hz and in mono, the sounds are definitely ADPCM/DSP encoded, and there's four distinct chunks in the audio files.

The first chunk is the header and Sound Table, that lists all the sounds that are in the AGSC file. There's a second chunk that currently unknown (possibly loop points or other miscellaneous info) and after that, there's a third chunk that has all the ADPCM/DSP streams in it. Finally, there's a fourth chunk that my friends and I believe maybe the offsets for the streams. This last chunk is always loaded into the Gamecube's RAM.
