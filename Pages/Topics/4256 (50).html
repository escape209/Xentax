Post by Nobby » Thu Sep 30, 2010 9:42 pm

CityPoke912 wrote:I converted BMW M5 to GTA4. The mesh is converted, but textures interior are missing. Are they on a Forza 3 disk and in which folder?
To answer this for you and anyone else that seems to struggle. NO they are not on disk 3 everything you need for the car is in the Zip files on disk 1. The same zip as you extracted the car model and the exterior textures from. When you extract the zip file, you should have something like this..
  • 2 folders one named Livery masks and one names physics.
    2 XML documents named "carattribs" and "shadersettings"
    1 HTML build report
    Approximately 10 .Carbin files ( models )
    Approximately 10 .XDS files ( textures )
For the .Carbin models you should have AT LEAST! 4 rotor models, 4 calliper models, a main model for the body in your case here bmw_m5_09.carbin, an LOD0 version of that car and a cockpit model.

For the textures you should have AT LEAST! 4 images for lights, a lights and a lights_lod0 for both damaged and non damaged. 4 images for the exterior, again 2 for damaged and 2 for non damaged.
And finally a " interior_lod0.xds " file THIS is the interior textures.

BTW i just unzipped the BMW M5 myself and ALL of those files have been extracted ( including the Interior_lod0.xds ) so it must be a user error rather than a software error that you are experiencing.
