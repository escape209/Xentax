TomWin wrote:file names and folders structure is screw up in TDFRL and C.A.R.S. after unpack bff, can it be fixed?
Does xbox version of TDF have the same problem?
SMS has changed the structure of the BFF files in both C.A.R.S. and TDFRL. The BFF files no longer contain standard file or folder names, as it did in the past with Shift 1 and S2U. The Xbox version is the same. So, you're left with the task of renaming all of the files, which is fairly straightforward (if you've worked on cars in either Shift 1 or S2U in the past).
