So I learned something tonight. Despite the fact that most of the Sweepers are just different colors (and I quickly took care of the other two, one being a texture-swap, other other taking a little more time)... changing the file name from "av__" (sweeper) to say "cv__" (custom sweeper) or "je__" (death machine) won't work as one would expect. I found that while the models look fine... the enemies don't act right. I encountered "Custom Sweeper", and it just stood around not attacking for a while, and when it did attack, and "Smoke Shot" appeared on screen..... well, "smoke shot" was not the attack it used, and "Matra Magic" failed to do anything.

Not entirely sure of the reason behind that....
