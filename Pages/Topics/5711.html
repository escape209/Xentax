If you really want to host your models somewhere, why not do it the easy way and not need to worry about involving websites with possible legalities. Just upload all the files to a host service like megaupload, fileserve or any of the other hundred host sites. then just make a post on here or any other forum, and just link to the files. that way your not actually hosting anything on say... Xentax's servers so they cannot get sued for legal reasons. Most file hosts allow you to have a " folder" so you can keep all your files in one place rather than scattered all over the internet. but if you want to post on a website then probably Models Resource or GFXworld are your best places.

Just out of interest. the " 25" in your name, is that supposed to represent your age. coz to be honest most of your posts that ive seen are really silly questions that a kid would ask. ANYONE getting involved in illegal activities ( which includes reverse engineering games to access models ) should know what laws exists regarding the topic of question BEFORE doing the activity in question. Posting copyright protected models/ games is no different to posting copyright protected music ( you do NOT do it on mainstream websites )

Please kid, in future THINK about what your asking before you hit the "post" button. what we are doing here IS illegal, we are breaking into games to steal things ( models/textures/audio files ) so there are only certain places you can announce that. You could probably announce it on facebook as long as you dont go shouting " heres the latest models i stole from a game" You could try something a little more tactful like " a quick pic of some of my latest models ive been working on, click here to DL them" when you refer to them as " your models" and " working on" it makes it sound like you had a go at making the models in a 3d editor rather than stealing them, so your less likely to get the post removed by the mods.
