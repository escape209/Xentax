Post by MrAdults » Fri Feb 25, 2011 12:36 pm

mariokart64n wrote:files were compressed I think..
Most data is not compressed. Extract the pkg with Noesis 2.85 and it will sort the files into folders for you. Look at arcs/*.pack files and you'll see many files (file0329.pack, 30, 31, etc) have model data right there plain as day. MDL headers with visible surface/material strings and tons of plainly visible vertex data. Should be pretty easy to make a model importer for the format.
