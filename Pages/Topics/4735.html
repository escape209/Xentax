I don't have any files from GOW 2, so I can't be sure of that format so if you could upload so of the larger .WAD files from the disc that would be good. In the GOW 1 Hydra demo, I know that at least some models are in the WAD files, but I also think that WAD have compressed sections, and other files are compressed too.
