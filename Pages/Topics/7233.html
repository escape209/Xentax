DOTAPRINCE wrote:Thanks, so are the spiky meshes natural? They are the bones but its a little distracting seeing it and the model itself can't be seen. How do I fix that?
If ur using tha PSK importer, just make sure you uncheck tha "See thru mesh" option and if ur bones are bigger than your mesh, or just too big, give it a smaller number like 0.5 in tha "Bone Size" option.
If ur model is too small, give it a higher number, sometimes i gotta put tha " Mesh Scale" to 100 for some models.
After you import and ur bones are an ok size but think they are still in tha way just hide them in tha "Display" tab in 3d max.
