Post by Straight Edge » Mon Oct 05, 2020 8:46 am

When you extract the character model from the GEAR_Assets container, it gives you a folder with the textures inside. the CTP shows you the colors used for the character skins in the game. like say if I wanted to get the correct colors for Baraka's default skin (SKIN_A), i'd look for "BAR_001_CPT0". Its a very small image with colors that you'd use as a reference for manually painting the SKIN_A texture in photoshop.
