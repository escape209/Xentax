Post by SLIFallen » Tue Dec 28, 2010 2:20 am

I got this game to work (show the missing characters) with 3DRipper by UNCHECKING every option EXCEPT "show textures in viewport" (so it will still show the textures)

In the limited testing I have done thus far, I have not found a combination of settings in 3dripper that will still get the characters..but NOT the hud and a whole lot of extra volume meshes (lots of domes and planar meshes and what not) So you have a bit of cleaning up to do, but it will get the characters this way. Not in a nice default pose mind you like an asset extractor/importer would do. (no such thing for this game BTW) But it's better than nothing!
