Actually, my problem was silly. I didn't know that Ferrari FF is called F142 in forza .

Looking up to your issue, I think you have not unpacked all.
After extracting things with exiso, there are some packages to extract, especially on DVD2. They are in folder "content", 4 files called for example "4d53091000000001". You have to open them in wxpirs, extract, then extract newly unpacked big zip files with bms script. Inside those zips you will find missing cars and much more. Good luck.
