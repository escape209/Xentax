Post by Mr.Mouse » Fri Aug 27, 2004 10:08 pm

Here we go with the pictures of my beloved VIC20 (anno 1982).



The front side of the original box.

"Met kleur en muziek!" (With colour and music)



The back side of the box.

"An expandable computer system" which was literally (and therefore grammatically unstable) translated to Dutch: "Een met uitbreidingsmogelijkheden computer systeem".


The trusty VIC20 itself! Notice the nice colours, emphasizing the Colour Computer.


The whole set-up, with original tapedeck. Did you know the VIC only had one joystick port? Now you do.

Check out that TV screen as well, notice how it has 22 columns. Imagine going through a listing showing in lines that have 22 characters per line. Luckily that it had only 3.5 BASIC KRam standard. I got it with an 16K expansion cartridge though...THAT'S LIKE 6 times MORE MAN!!!!!!
