Sharppy wrote: ↑Fri Apr 09, 2021 11:57 pmI really just go by the patterns of data from Model Researcher which mariokart64 said was a bad thing to do.
Depends on what you mean by "the patterns of data". Using search-patterns in a hex editor to get "an idea of the data structure" is not that bad, if you have experience with some dozens of 3D formats.
It's the fastest way to get results I know of. Not everybody has the time/patience/knowledge to do a full format analysis.

btw: FVF= flexible vertex format
