You can disable lines truncate, it is defined in jit\bc.lua
Simply change

Code: Select all

kc = format(#kc > 40 and '"%.40s"~' or '"%s"', gsub(kc, "%c", ctlsub))
to

Code: Select all

kc = format('"%s"', gsub(kc, "%c", ctlsub))
