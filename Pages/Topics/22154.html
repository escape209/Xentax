Post by ardivian » Wed Jun 29, 2022 7:23 pm

New to this, but after a few/way too hours of research and trying things, I figured out that once you process using the file above to get the SAB from the Unreal assets you can open them in Foobar as long as you have the vgmstream component installed for it. It will process the SAB and you can see all the goodies inside of it. Yuffie's vo-asset had 331 audio files that I was able to convert to mp3 as a test.

Sorry for the necro, but I wanted to post this info in case someone from the Steam release gets the same idea after hearing Yuffie hum the victory fanfare. Trying to use cmd prompt for vgmstream test.exe was only extracting a single 22kb of audio from the voice over SABs I extracted, so then I went down the rabbit hole.
