Milkshape SUCKs.... its an over glorified model viewer, with limited editing functions. these functions arent even decent enough to call MS3D a 3D editor. Use Blender, its free and is more capable then milkshape. I know people complain about the GUI, but your stupid if you don't take advantage of such a powerful tool (and one that is free)

I don't know why people still use MS3D, it hasn't been updated in years. and you'll get little plugin support as its not as community driven as something like blender or 3dsmax

basically what you see is what you get with milkshape, it has a large format support list. but all these are dated, and hardly have a use in modern modding applications
