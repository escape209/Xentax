Post by friendsofwatto » Thu Feb 23, 2006 10:47 am

Actually I don't keep my upcoming things secret, I do it for a very good reason. If I say something like "Game Extractor 2.0 will have magical powers", then it ties me down to something that I might not be able to do for some reason. For example, I might not be able to do this for a while due to lack of time, or other more important things to implement might come up beforehand. If I lock myself in to adding "magic powers" in the next GE, then I can't really release the next version until that is in it.

Plus, as soon as you say something about an upcoming version, people jsut keep emailing you constantly saying "when will it be ready" - I don't really want that.

So, its not that I want to keep it secret, its actually just for my own benefit. If you dig hard enough, you can probably find my GE ToDo list floating around somewhere, but I always seem to keep adding more things to the list without actually removing anything from it. Ironically, adding the File Cutter into GE is something that I have had on my list for a while, but obviously I havn't done that yet
