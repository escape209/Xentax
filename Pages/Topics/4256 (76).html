StillDre wrote:Hi, i'm new here and don't quite understand where to download what, to could extract from fm3/fm4 .iso? I want to use some circuite textures and some models of it from FM3 and some cars from FM4.. Thx in advance
First off all, welcome to the forum.

To extract .iso, you'll need:
http://konsolenprofis.de/downloads/download.php?id=1499
Scroll down till you see the red Download.

As you see, this topic has lots of pages.
FM4 is just a few months out and there hasn't been figured out the most things.
The only thing that are supported are the lod0 models.
That's all, the other big problems are the UV-mappings.
On some models, like Autovista, the UV-mapping is a bit right, but it needs huge scaling.
And other models, like the normal models when racing, have screwed up UV-mappings.
The only thing to fix the mapping is doing it ourself.
But as you see, the models contains lots of objects.

About the circuite textures, you can try this:
http://codeescape.com/2011/01/forza-2-a ... tion-tool/

Good luck
