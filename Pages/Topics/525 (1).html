Post by lionheartuk » Tue Jul 11, 2006 7:03 pm

It seems to me that the textures on the LEFT are the ones you see UP CLOSE, thus the Quality is much higher, but this means it uses up MORE memory.
The ones on the RIGHT however seem to be LOWER QUALITY textures, this could be because (this theory makes the most sense) using LOADSA of HIGH QUALITY textures on far away enemies and building is really a waste of memory, you cant see anything in detail so the things you wouldn't see are omitted, and also the lower quality textures use up less memory, so this means that you can see more without slowing anything down framerate wise.
I'm not sure if the PS2 version was done using this approach but I'm pretty confident that this is the reason behind why there are duplicates of every file.
