Post by ajmiles » Sat Apr 10, 2010 11:02 pm

The fiesta has:

damage, damage_lod0
damagelights, damagelights_lod0
interior_lod0
leather2_nrm
lights, lights_lod0
nodamage, nodamage_lod0
plastic2_nrm

The BMW doesn't appear to have normal maps for leather or plastic.
The Aston Martin I have has a 'wood' too and again no normal maps. So it varies per car.

Bear in mind that the cars in game are coloured rather than textured, and any designs are done using their fancy decal system, which we aren't going to be able to reproduce.
