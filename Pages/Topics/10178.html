Code: Select all

< Hex > | < Text/ASCII >
42494E5652534E7B0D0A2020202020202020310D0A7D0D0A4D455348686561645F6D6F757468007B565254587BDC0E00 | BINVRSN{..        1..}..MESHhead_mouth.{VRTX{...
