Mr.Mouse recently locked a thread about Max Payne 3, because the game wasn't released yet, however I do have certain questions and I believe they should be stated publically.

1. What about games that are preloaded on Steam, several days, sometimes two weeks before the release date? You get the files to toy around with even if you can't run the game yet -- and you get said files legally -- so why not discuss these?

2. What about games that have public, or semi-public beta tests with normally downloadable beta clients -- are they also off the chart?

michalss was wrong on many levels -- about scene groups' sources for games, which often are in the companies themselves, only that their bosses don't know it, and most importantly about the availability of Max Payne 3 PC -- which is, by what I saw, unavailable on warez sites, at least in PC flavor which I've noticed was one of the versions discussed there. True, X360 version was warezzed out already, but the only source for PC files I can possibly think of is Steam preload (as the game is coming to steam pretty soon).

EDIT: I just looked into the screenshots and saw that they show files from the X360 build of MP3 so obviously there are no PC files discussed, so albeit the MP3-specific question flies out the window, the remaining ones still remain:

- what about Steam preloads and official (non-leaked) betas?
