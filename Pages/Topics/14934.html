@majidemo
first of all the notation of your faces in the obj file is incomplete.
It writes
f 1 2 3
f 1 3 4
[...]

Should be
f 1/1/1 2/2/2 3/3/3
f 1/1/1 3/3/3 4/4/4
[...]

because the 3D apps I know can't create the uv map from f 1 2 3 etc.
