Maxnut23 wrote:I can get objects and textures but the textures are not lining up. Just wondering if there is a fix for that yet?
I think it's a bonus functionality in addition to primary goal - convert car models, so it's feature has very limited support by author...
Justdragos wrote:Thank you Andrakann, but how do i extract the sounds from the game? Enginesounds.....?
You can use this tool for conversion to wav. Put towav.exe and ConvAll.bat from archive into one folder with forza sound banks .fsb(s) and run ConvAll.bat.
One .fsb contains multiple (10-15) sound samples, so put one in empty folder before running .bat, or you'll be overwhelmed by tons of .wav-s =)
