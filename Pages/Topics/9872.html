The format looks like this (wavefront-obj):

# 1512 verts
# 1. 0x8fc:
v 0.012800 -0.899100 0.161500
v 0.013300 -0.930900 0.144100
v 0.015300 -0.891000 0.120800
v 0.032800 1.290900 -0.125500
...
# 0x4fdc

# 0xDD9C, 988 faces
f 1 2 3
f 4 5 6
f 7 8 9
f 10 11 12
f 13 14 15
...
f 1507 1508 1509
f 1510 1511 1512
# 0x152cc:
# face index min/max: 1 / 1512
