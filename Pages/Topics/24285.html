It seems that your PAC files are supported by HedgeLib https://github.com/Radfordhound/HedgeLib
You can download latest build here https://ci.appveyor.com/project/Radford ... /artifacts


And you can use it like this:

Code: Select all

HedgeArcPack.exe -T=rio -E pea_anim_gymcln_BRZ.pac out_dir

Check also this article http://wiki.xentax.com/index.php/Sonic_Games_PAC
