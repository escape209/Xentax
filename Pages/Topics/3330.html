Ok i just double checked, Milkshape comes with the plugin "Kratisto's Half Life MDL Decompiler" but it only supports 1 file at a time , i strongly suggest getting The HLSDK which as mentioned above comes with all the Model sources..

Milkshape is here http://chumbalum.swissquake.ch/ms3d/download.html

HL SDK 2.3 is here http://games.on.net/file/358/Half-life_SDK

As for Half Life 2, there is only 1 decompiler that i know of, and believe me i've searched for others (with batch support)
That's http://www.chaosincarnate.net/cannonfod ... mpiler.php

Version 0.5 will support most HL2 engine games, including mods.
However for something like Team Fortress 2, you need to download the older version, Version 0.4.1 in order to decompile its models.

For something like Left 4 Dead, well i won't go into detail, but Alot of work needs to be done in order to decompile those models
